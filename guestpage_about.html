Our Story

Tandikan Tri Team is more than just an athletic club; it is a vibrant community dedicated to the spirit of endurance and the pursuit of personal excellence. Inspired by the resilience and beauty of Palawan’s iconic peacock-pheasant, our team brings together swimmers, cyclists, and runners of all skill levels...
